## Beale Street Theater Website Plan Overview

This redesign focuses on three priorities:

1. Making it easier to buy tickets without leaving the website  
2. Highlighting upcoming performances clearly  
3. Showcasing the theater’s community impact

Key Improvements:

• Automatic homepage image slider featuring performances and venue photos • Large calendar at the top of the Shows page • Dedicated show pages with optional video trailers • Embedded Ludus ticket system so patrons complete purchases without leaving the site • Clear Buy Tickets buttons across the site • Youth Theater and Workshops highlighted • Sponsorship and donation pages simplified with clear options • Mobile-friendly layout

Ticketing Experience: Visitors will:

1. Click a show from the calendar  
2. View show details  
3. Click Buy Tickets  
4. Complete purchase inside our website via embedded Ludus system

Support Section Improvements: • Pay-It-Forward Ticket Program • Adopt-A-Seat campaign • Sponsor tiers clearly displayed

Visit Page: • Map and directions • Restaurant and bar recommendations

The goal is clarity, simplicity, and improved ticket conversions while maintaining the theater’s community-centered identity.
